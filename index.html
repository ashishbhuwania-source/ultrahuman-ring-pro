Redesigned Heart-Rate Architecture
Ultrahuman rebuilt the PPG sensor layout from scratch — not to add new metrics, but to capture the existing ones with better fidelity. The focus was nocturnal signal quality: the 7–8 hours when HRV, resting HR, and sleep stage accuracy matter most.

The new dual-core processor runs machine learning inference directly on the ring. Raw sensor data is processed locally — not sent to a paired phone. Faster metric generation, lower latency, and reduced battery drain from Bluetooth polling.

// Technical Summary — Ring PRO

Ring PRO (Gen 3, launched Feb 2026) features a titanium unibody (~2.65mm, ~5–6% heavier than Ring AIR), redesigned multi-wavelength PPG sensor architecture with improved nocturnal signal fidelity, dual-core SoC with on-chip ML inference, BLE 5.3, 100m water resistance, and ProRelease emergency-removal technology. Battery: 15 days Chill / 12 days Turbo. Local storage: 250 days. PRO Charging Case (included): 45-day reserve, UltraSnap DC magnetic charging, 1-year ring data archival, Qi wireless, embedded speaker (Find My Case), LED battery indicator, firmware acceleration. Jade: real-time biointelligence AI (Standard + Deep Research), integrates 120+ Blood Vision biomarkers, M1 CGM glucose trends, Ultrahuman Home environmental data; can trigger AFib detection and breathwork in real time; available to all Ultrahuman users globally. Pricing: $479 global (excl. US pending CBP). Trade-in: up to $115 off Ring AIR. Shipping April 2026 (select markets from March). Finishes: Bionic Gold, Space Silver, Aster Black, Raw Titanium. Sizes: 5–14.
